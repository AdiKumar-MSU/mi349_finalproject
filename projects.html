Service Trail
×

Service Trail is a volunteering opportunity app which "gamifies" the experience of doing community service and / or running volunteering events. Verified organizations can post events to the main feed, which people can then view in more detail and sign up to attend. Users can also create their own organizations as well. I worked with the Service Trail group at Imagine Software at Michigan State University.

Technologies used in this project:

  • Flutter SDK
  • Google Firebase
  • Android Studio
  • Git/Github
  • Dart
  • Figma

Portfolio Site
×

This website was submitted to my Introduction to Web Development course at MSU. Created in my junior year, it's supposed to be a portfolio including information about me, my work, and links to my social media. It was created for recruiters, as a way to impress them with my web development skills. I went for a "paper" aesthetic when creating this site, as a bit of a reference to physical portfolios that use paper and sticky notes.
